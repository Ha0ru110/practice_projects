  • Facts about musk turtles

The icon for this webpage is a turtle

It is a musk turtle to be precise, and it is one of the most common pet turtles around the world

It is an very interesting animal

It can breathe through its tongue and be underwater for an infinite amount of time

Other interesting facts

general species facts:

  1. ...Latin species name is "Sternotherus Odoratus"
  2. ...belongs to the reptile family along with fish, birds,    snakes and other turtle species
  3. ...many turtle species are in danger of extinction    today, but this one's not under any threat
